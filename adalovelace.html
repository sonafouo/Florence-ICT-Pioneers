The First Programmer

Biography

Ada Lovelace was a brilliant mathematician and the first computer programmer. She wrote the first algorithm for Charles Babbage's Analytical Engine, a prototype of a digital computer.

She also foresaw the potential of computers to perform more than just calculations.

Key Contributions

  • Wrote the first algorithm intended to be processed by a machine
  • Recognized the potential of computers beyond mere calculation
  • Contributed to the field of computer science before it even existed
